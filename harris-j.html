Biography

Harris J was born in London, England on May 2, 1997. British-Muslim recording artist who won the Awakening Talent Contest in 2013, leading him to sign a record deal with Awakening Records. Through Awakening, he released his first official music video for the song “Salam Alaikum” as part of his debut album Salam. He and Amy Winehouse are both alumni of the BRIT School of Performing Arts. He was singing in public by the age of five, performing at school recitals and multi-faith events. He trained as a football player at the Elite Training Club at the Chelsea Academy when he was twelve years old.
