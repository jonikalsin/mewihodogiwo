Biography

Jeff Matika was born in Texas on April 21, 1971. Best recognized as a touring guitarist and backing vocalist for the long-running popular alt-rock band Green Day. He began touring with the band in 2009. Both he and Jason Freese are long-running touring members of Green Day. Hailing from Little Rock Arkansas, he was friends with Jason White before joining Green Day. He was previously a member of Ashtray Babyhead and Magic Cropdusters.
