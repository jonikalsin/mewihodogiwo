Jonathan Taylor Thomas Spotted for the First Time in Years!

Publish date: 2024-05-16

Jonathan Taylor Thomas has been spotted in public for the first time in years!

The 39-year-old former actor is best known for his role as Randy Taylor on the sitcom Home Improvement and for voicing the role of young Simba in Disney’s animated The Lion King movie.

It has been nearly eight years since anyone has photographed Jonathan in public, though he did make guest appearances on former co-star Tim Allen‘s show Last Man Standing between 2013 and 2015.

You can see the new photos of JTT by going to TooFab‘s website.

Jonathan previously explained his decision to step back from acting and to attend college at Harvard University and Columbia University.

“I’d been going nonstop since I was 8 years old. I wanted to go to school, to travel and have a bit of a break,” he said in the past. “To sit in a big library amongst books and students, that was pretty cool. It was a novel experience for me.”
