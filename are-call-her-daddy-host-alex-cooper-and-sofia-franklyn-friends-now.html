Are Call Her Daddy Host Alex Cooper And Sofia Franklyn Friends Now?

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Sofia Franklyn and Alex Cooper are well-known for hosting Call Her Daddy, advice and comedy podcast that they co-founded in 2018.

However, the two pals split up in the late spring of 2020 after a public feud with Barstool Sports founder David Portnoy. The podcast was owned and distributed by Barstool until June 2021.

The public feud between the two eventually led to Franklyn leaving the podcast and Cooper hosting the show on her own.

Sofia Franklyn and Alex Cooper Aren’t Getting Along Anytime Soon

Since Franklyn and Cooper broke their relationship, people have been questioning if they would ever see them together again. Not only that, but fans want to know if they are still friends.

When she appeared on an episode of The Morning Toast in February 2022, the former Call Her Daddy co-host addressed all of those questions. She hinted that the former coworkers and best friends are unlikely to rekindle their friendship.

During the chat, Franklyn even quipped that she might rethink working with Cooper if the current Call Her Daddy host offered her $30 million. She was referring to Cooper’s $60 million deal with Spotify, which is set to begin in 2021.

“It was also difficult to watch something you’d worked on for so [expletive] long being sold for that kind of money,”

Franklyn remarked on the Spotify/Call Her Daddy agreement. She went on to say,

“I genuinely think if I had continued on the program and we hadn’t deceived each other… I honestly believe we could have sold it for more money, which was always our intention.”

To cut to the chase, Franklyn’s statements make it clear that she and Cooper are not friends, and they have no plans to reconcile.

Sofia Franklyn Launches Her Own Podcast

While Cooper kept hosting the Call Her Daddy podcast, Franklyn launched her own, Sofia With an F.

In addition to where she is now in her work, the internet star told The Morning Toast presenter Claudia Oshry that she was in a “90 percent better place.”

Franklyn also added that she would be lying if she said she was completely back to who she was when she worked at Call Her Daddy.

Cooper, on the other hand, appears to be doing exceptionally well with the long-running show. Her most recent episode, starring former adult actress Julia Fox, is also doing incredibly well.

During the conversation, Fox revealed her experiences in the adult industry as well as her thoughts on her boyfriend Kanye West, who is still in love with his separated wife, Kim Kardashian.
