Is Ryan Armour related to Tommy Armour?

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Ryan Armour is an American professional golfer who is the proud winner of one PGA Tour and one Korn Ferry Tour.

Is Ryan Armour related to Tommy Armour?

Armour is a known name in the Golf world. Another holder of this name is none other than Tommy Armour. However, the two men are not related, they only share the same last name.

Tommy Armour joined the PGA Tour in 1981 at the age of 21.

During his career, he has had two career PGA Tour victories, as well as winning the 1990 Phoenix Open and the 2003 Valero Texas Open.

His scoring record for the best overall 72-hole score, which he set with his Valero Texas Open victory in 2003, was surpassed in 2017 by Justin Thomas.

Tommy Armour in addition to being an accomplished golfer also has golf in his blood. His grandfather is the three-time major champion Tommy Armour.
