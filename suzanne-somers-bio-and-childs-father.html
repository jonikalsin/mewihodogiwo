Suzanne Somers: Bio and Childs Father

Publish date: 2024-05-11

Suzanne Somers, born as Suzanne Marie Mahoney on October 16, 1946, was an American actress, author, singer, businesswoman, and health spokesperson. She was best known for her television roles of Chrissy Snow on Three’s Company (1977–1981) and Carol Foster Lambert on Step by Step (1991–1998).

Early Life

Born in San Bruno, California, Suzanne was the third of four children in a working-class Irish-American Catholic family.

Her mother, Marion Elizabeth (née Turner), was a medical secretary, and her father, Francis Mahoney, was a laborer and gardener.

Career

Somers began her acting career in small roles during the late 1960s and early 1970s.

She gained fame for her role in the television show Three’s Company. Later, she played Carol Foster Lambert on the TV series Step by Step.
