Career

Stephanie Sigman debuted her career in 2006 with her short film “Con lujo de detalle”. She got a breakthrough by appearing in the award-winning 2011 film “Miss Bala”. She landed the character “Catalina Aguado” in the documentary film “Flight of the Butterflies” in 2012 and as “Maria Salatzar” in the 2013 thriller drama film “Pioneer”.

She was also part of “Once Upon a Time in Venice”, “Shimmer Lake”, “War on Everyone”, “El Cielo es Azul”, “Morelos”, “Rio de Oro” and many more films.

Furthermore, she portrayed the fictional character “Estrella” in the James Bond series spy film “Spectre” in 2015 and the role of “Sister Charlotte” in the horror-thriller film “Annabelle: Creation” in 2017. She has also made her appearance in several popular television series. She assumed as a recurring role of “Eva Guerra” in the American crime drama television series “The Bridge” in 2013-2014 and the main role of “Valeria Velez” in the Netflix crime drama series “Narcos” in 2015-2016.

“The Arrangement”, “Presence” is also included in her television projects. She acted a recurring role of “Monica Ava” in the American anthology true crime television series “American Crime” in 2016 and the main role of a commanding officer of LAPD Metropolitan Division “Jessica Cortez” in the action fiction drama series “S.W.A.T.” from 2017 to 2019. In 2014 she was also featured in the Arctic Monkeys music video for “Snap Out of It”.
